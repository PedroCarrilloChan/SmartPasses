Privacy Policy

Smart Passes Wallet App

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Passes Wallet ("we," "our," "us," or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play Store.

Smart Passes Wallet is designed to display and organize your digital passes, loyalty cards, coupons, tickets, and memberships. We are committed to protecting your privacy and we do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Smart Passes Wallet is designed with your privacy as a priority. We want to be completely transparent: our App does not collect any user data.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. How the App Works

Smart Passes Wallet functions entirely on your device (locally). Here's how it works:

  • Local Storage Only: All digital passes and cards you add to the App are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone.
  • No Server Communication: The App does not transmit any of your pass data or personal information to external servers.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.
  • Offline Functionality: The App works completely offline. No internet connection is required to view your passes.

4. Third-Party Services

Smart Passes Wallet does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising networks or ad tracking
  • Crash reporting tools that collect personal data
  • Social media login or integration
  • Any other third-party data collection services

5. Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us, you maintain complete control over your information. We recommend that you:

  • Use your device's built-in security features (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)
  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Be cautious when adding passes from unknown sources

6. Children's Privacy

Smart Passes Wallet does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, there are no special provisions needed for children's privacy. The App is safe for users of all ages.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. All data stored by the App is under your complete control on your device. You can:

  • Delete individual passes at any time within the App
  • Clear all App data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data from your device

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

If we make any material changes to this policy, we will notify users through the App or by other appropriate means.

9. Compliance with Google Play Requirements

This Privacy Policy is provided in compliance with Google Play Store requirements. We confirm that:

  • This policy accurately describes our data practices
  • The App's Data Safety section in Google Play accurately reflects that no user data is collected
  • We will update this policy and the Data Safety section if our practices change

11. Summary

In Summary:

  • We do NOT collect any personal data
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • All your passes are stored locally on your device only
  • You have complete control over your data
